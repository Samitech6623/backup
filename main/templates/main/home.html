Programmes Offered in Our School

EdenField Academy provides a nurturing, stimulating, and playful environment for children in their early years.

We offer a holistic approach to early childhood education, focusing on emotional, social, cognitive, and physical development through our four foundational programmes: Daycare, Playgroup, Pre-Primary 1, and Pre-Primary 2.

Daycare Programme

Our daycare provides a safe, warm, and caring environment where infants and toddlers are lovingly cared for throughout the day.

With trained caregivers and age-appropriate activities, children develop trust, security, and basic motor and communication skills through play-based learning.

Playgroup Programme

Our playgroup classes introduce children to structured routines, interactive play, music, and storytelling.

Through social interaction and creative exploration, children begin to build early language and social skills, laying the foundation for lifelong learning in a fun, supportive environment.

Pre-Primary 1

In Pre-Primary 1, children begin more structured learning experiences in literacy, numeracy, and creative expression.

Our curriculum encourages curiosity and independent thinking through hands-on activities, storytelling, and group learning.

Pre-Primary 2

Pre-Primary 2 is designed to prepare children for primary school. We focus on foundational literacy and numeracy skills, as well as critical thinking and problem-solving through interactive lessons and collaborative learning.

Emotional and social development continues to be a key focus as we build confident and school-ready learners.

Why Choose EdenField Academy?

At EdenField, we believe in the power of early education to shape young minds. Our passionate team, child-friendly facilities, and creative curriculum offer the perfect start for every child's learning journey.
