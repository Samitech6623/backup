About Our School

Who We Are

EdenField Kindergarten is a nurturing early learning center located in Muchatha, Kiambu County — between Ruaka and Banana.
We are dedicated to helping children grow through love, play, and structured learning.
We believe that the foundation of a happy, successful life begins in the early years — and we're here to make every moment count.

Our experienced and compassionate teachers are the heart of our learning community.
Each educator is carefully selected not only for their qualifications but also for their passion for early childhood development.
Our classrooms are safe, vibrant, and thoughtfully designed to inspire curiosity and creativity.
With small class sizes, personalized attention, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere, we ensure every child feels seen, supported, and empowered to learn at their own pace.
The combination of nurturing staff and a stimulating environment makes EdenField the perfect place for young minds to grow and thrive.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision: To be a place where every child feels safe, inspired, and excited to learn.

Our Mission: To support children's growth through a balanced blend of play, creativity, social interaction, and early academics.

Our Core Values

Care & Safety
We provide a warm, secure environment where every child is loved and protected.
Creativity
We encourage imagination through music, art, storytelling, and exploration.
Curiosity
We nurture children's natural desire to explore, question, and learn.
Community
We value teamwork and support between families, teachers, and children.

Our Programmes

Daycare
Caring for children in a loving, play-based environment while parents are away.
Playgroup
Introducing routines, music, stories, and basic social skills through play.
Pre-Primary 1
Developing early literacy, numeracy, and independence through hands-on learning.
Pre-Primary 2
Preparing children for primary school with strong foundations in reading, math, and confidence.
