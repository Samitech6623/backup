Our Classes

At EdenField Kindergarten, we offer age-appropriate, engaging, and nurturing classes designed to support your child's development at every stage.

Baby Class (2-3 years)

A gentle introduction to school life through guided play, storytelling, music, and sensory activities.

Playgroup (3-4 years)

Focuses on social development, early language skills, fine motor activities, and interactive play.

Pre-Primary 1 (PP1) - Ages 4-5

Builds foundational skills in numeracy, literacy, art, and environmental awareness. Aligned with Kenya's CBC curriculum.

Pre-Primary 2 (PP2) - Ages 5-6

Prepares learners for Grade 1 with a focus on structured learning, problem-solving, creativity, and communication.
